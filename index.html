Slow Down, Think Before You Scan

🚫

Unknown Sources

Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources.

🔗

Verify the URL

If unsure, contact the sender or official source.

🔒

Protect Your Information

Never share sensitive data or access payment accounts through suspicious QR codes.
